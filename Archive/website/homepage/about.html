Our Inspiration

Why We Built Ovify

Ovify was inspired by the challenges of navigating complex, unsupported IVF cycles outside the clinic.

During our founder's early cycles, she faced extreme isolation and administrative chaos. Despite advanced medical care, managing complex medication schedules, jargon, and emotional spikes between clinic visits was unsupported. In her third cycle, a single missed dose led to premature ovulation and a devastating cancellation.

Determined to build a better path, she prioritized nervous-system safety. In her fourth cycle, she implemented a structured regimen of yoga, mindfulness, breathing, and clinical precision—resulting in a successful cycle with 10 eggs and 2 healthy tested embryos.

From this, Ovify was born to bridge the gap between clinical instruction and patient execution, extending clinical care directly into the home so no one has to navigate the journey alone.

The Founder's Promise

"No woman should ever have to go through IVF the way she did — not alone, not confused, not unsupported, and not crying on the floor after a failed cycle with no one to guide her."

Technical Leadership

Rooted in Enterprise & AI Architecture

Ovify's foundation is built by blending medical robotics engineering, high-scale enterprise architecture, and state-of-the-art AI systems.

Our founder, Samaneh Khaleghi, has spent over a decade leading digital transformation and architecture strategies in highly regulated global environments. Her experience spans building single sign-on systems for millions of banking customers to designing medical imaging software for disease diagnosis at Imperial College London.

By combining her deep technical roots in Generative AI, cloud security, and medical imaging with her personal journey, she designed Ovify to offer clinics the robust, compliant, and secure digital infrastructure required to guide patients through complex IVF cycles safely.
