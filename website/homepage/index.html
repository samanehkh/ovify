Our Offering

What We Offer

A unified companion ecosystem that guides the patient, involves the support, and streamlines clinic operations.

Unified Care Ecosystem

Ovify integrates the patient companion, support network, and clinic dashboard in a real-time loop to automate compliance oversight.

  • ✓ Continuous Data Sync: Patient injections, alerts, and adjustments sync instantly.
  • ✓ Reduced Clinical Load: Connects support networks automatically to coordinate care tasks.
  • ✓ Proactive Risk Triage: Feeds real-time patient adherence warnings directly to the clinic.

1. Patient App

White-labeled mobile application that guides patients day-by-day, turning clinic prescriptions into stress-free scheduled actions.

  • ✓ Daily Protocol Roadmap: Turns complex clinical prescriptions into structured, scheduled checklists and injection guides.
  • ✓ CalmSeed Emotional AI: Rhythmical voice analysis and stress-screening with guided respiration to manage pre-injection anxiety.
  • ✓ Smart Reminders: Persistent alerts that adapt to user behavior to prevent missed or late injections.

2. Support App

A collaborative space that involves partners, family, or friends in the fertility journey, sharing the burden and eliminating confusion.

  • ✓ Coordinated Action Lists: Shared checklists ensure everyone knows exactly how and when to assist.
  • ✓ Empathy Tips & Prompts: Timely, scientifically backed notifications helping support networks coordinate daily care tasks.
  • ✓ Real-time Adherence Log: Support members receive immediate notifications when doses are checked off, keeping everyone synced.

3. Clinic Dashboard

Predictive AI dashboard that tracks patient adherence and flags cycle dropout risks in real time.

  • ✓ AI-Enabled Triage Escalation: Automatically identifies early compliance warning signals and alerts nurses before critical dosing windows are missed.
  • ✓ Predictive Dropout Analytics: Forecasts patient dropout risk using voice metrics, stress indices, and behavioral timelines to enable timely clinical support.
  • ✓ Active Adherence Overview: Instant dashboards of completed doses, stress patterns, and real-time patient status logs.
