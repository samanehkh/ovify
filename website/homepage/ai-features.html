Clinical Operations Engine

1. Clinical Triage Dashboard — Real Time AI Monitoring

The Clinical Triage Dashboard is the central command center for nursing teams, moving clinics from reactive patient communication to proactive, automated compliance oversight.

By consolidating patient app logs, voice stress analytics, and dose confirmations, the dashboard highlights compliance anomalies and dropout risks before they affect cycles.

  • Adherence Anomaly Detection: AI automatically flags missed doses, incorrect dosage amounts, and timing offsets.
  • Predictive Dropout Triage: Uses CalmSeed voice stress trends to highlight patients at risk of abandoning their treatment.
  • Auto-Triage Priority Queue: Ranks patient messages by urgency, letting nurses focus on clinical risks rather than repetitive admin.
  • Seamless EMR Integration: Synchronizes compliance history, stress data, and notes directly into patient records.

The Missing Human Layer

5. Emotional & Relationship Support AI — Care Alignment

Ovify automates patient-support care alignment during high-stress cycles. This engine provides emotional stabilization for the patient and coordination prompts for their support network.

  • Daily micro-messages: Context-aware emotional stabilizers responding to her protocol stage and CalmSeed stress score. ("You're doing something incredibly brave. Let's take today gently.")
  • Two-Week Wait Support Mode: A protective cocoon offering sleep support, grounding rituals, and pacing reminders to avoid obsessive symptom-checking.
  • Reflective Journaling (LittleLight AI): Transcribes voice or text journaling into a private emotional timeline, illustrated memory excerpts, and connection rituals.
  • Support AI: With consent, alerts the support regarding her feelings, how to avoid triggering language, and suggest one-minute support actions.
